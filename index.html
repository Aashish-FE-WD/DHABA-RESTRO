A dhaba is a roadside restaurant in the Indian subcontinent. They are on highways, generally serve local cuisine, and also serve as truck stops. Dhabas are a common feature on national and state highways.

The massive national highways that connect the cities are lined with some remarkable dhabas that have grown in popularity over time to become the destination's top tourist attractions.
